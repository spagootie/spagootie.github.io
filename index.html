How it plays

Simple to start, satisfying to master.

Players have access to three card types, each allowing for more varied and strategic gameplay:

01

Movement

Allow players to move cards, with bonus Movement+ cards allowing expanded movement.
02

Active

Cards that take effect instantly.
03

Passive

Equipable cards that activate upon capture.
